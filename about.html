About Quizzy

We're passionate about making learning fun, engaging, and rewarding. Our platform connects students and teachers in an innovative quiz-based learning environment.

Our Mission

To revolutionize education by making learning interactive in Landmark University, competitive, and enjoyable through our innovative quiz platform. We aim to empower students and teachers with tools that make knowledge acquisition engaging and rewarding.

Our Vision

To become the leading quiz platform where students worldwide can test their knowledge, compete with peers, and earn rewards while learning. We envision a future where education is gamified and accessible to everyone.
